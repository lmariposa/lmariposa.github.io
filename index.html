LEILAH GAIA

ABOUT ME

Musician, Songwriter

I was born and raised in Upstate New York. When I was seven I asked to play the upright bass. I was too small to play the bass and my grandfather, a luthier, had an extra ½ size cello that needed a home. So I started playing cello and then piano. I was constantly looking towards music to express myself. I wrote songs for piano and as time went by I started writing for the guitar. In high school, I began to focus on my voice. I attended Doane Stuart, a private college preparatory school in Albany, NY. At Doane, I began performing my music. I would sing on Tuesdays and Thursdays sometimes just for the high school and sometimes for the entire school. I loved performing and inspiring all of the younger students to express themselves through music.

My first gig outside of school was at a local Ice Cream Shop. I spent my summer performing for tips and pints of ice cream. Even though music consumed most of my life I still thought of it as a hobby. My highschool course schedule was designed so that I would be prepared to go straight into a career in Microbiology but one afternoon I took a leap step and decided I need to go to college for music. I auditioned for college without classical or technical training. I practiced my ass off and I sang for my future, from my heart. I am now an undergraduate at Berklee College of Music. I am studying music business, music engineering, and vocal performance.

Leilah Gaia Mariposa
